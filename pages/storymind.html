Why StoryMind exists

It started with one child.

StoryMind began with a real kid and a simple wish: a book that met her exactly where she was — her name, her interests, the specific things she was working on. Not a generic story with her name pasted in, but one built around her.

That idea grew into a tool for the people who do this work every day — parents at bedtime, teachers in the classroom, and speech-language pathologists shaping each session around a child's goals. Every book is generated fresh for one reader, with reading level, language, and therapy targets woven into the story itself.

It's still a personal project at heart. If it can help one more child feel seen in a story, it's doing its job.
