Private Property Intelligence

EquityIQ

Data scraped from Realtor.com via HomeHarvest for personal research. Value estimates are trimmed $/sqft medians from nearby townhome solds — a starting point, not an appraisal. Renovation tiers come from listing language; verify against photos (ask Claude to review any shortlisted property's photos). NACA reads are heuristics; only the HAND inspection is authoritative. Appreciation combines Zillow's zip-level index with this tool's own comps trend; past appreciation is not a guarantee. Monthly payment is a rough estimate at the NACA rate configured in homeharvest_pull.py — verify current rate at naca.com. The EquityIQ score (0–100) weighs price-vs-comps, renovation payback, zip appreciation, seller motivation, and days on market — use it to prioritize, not to decide.
